Cirriculum

The business curriculum will be customized to the cohort and individuals. Because we are giving access to members at various stages of development, everyone’s needs are different.

Therefore, we will customize our services for your particular space and time. With our vast network, in-house support, and resources, we offer solutions as they are needed now – not in theory.

There will be 90 daytime sessions. Every unit will have a 45-day mid-session review. After 90 days, you will be invited to attend the next session of 613lab. There are 4 sessions per year.

You can enter into 613 Lab at any beginning session period throughout the year. Our customized programming allows for things to be a little more flexible than our competitors.
