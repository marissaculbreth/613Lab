Our Services

Mentorship

Entrepreneurs are provided with mentors who best fit their needs. They are there to help guide and nurture the young entrepreneur on their path toward growth. The mentorship involves opening the door for making new connections and meeting new people. Ideally, the mentors may also end up serving as investors for the company.

Group Sessions Led by Top Leaders

The program includes access to a shared workspace with in-house entrepreneurs, workshops and more. The company provides group coaching sessions with top executives who deep dive into the concepts and business strategy presented by the entrepreneurs. These deep dives are curated to meet the needs of each individual company and its entrepreneur. Additionally, the meetings involve question and answer sessions, as well as the ability to interact with other entrepreneurs.

Personal Development Coaching

There is an immense amount of emotional obstacles one must overcome as an entrepreneur. The stress and difficulty of launching a startup is why approximately 80 percent of startups fail, according to Rabbi Joshua Kaller. The program uses personal development coaching to leverage wisdom and characteristics from people who have experience with successful startups. This coaching helps the company develop a strong mindset to ensure the longevity of their business.

International Network Access to Funding

613 Lab has mentors all over the world which creates many opportunities and connections for the participants. These international connections allow the entrepreneurs to gain insights from diverse groups of all backgrounds and experiences. Furthermore, 613 Lab does not provide funding initially. This motivates the new entrepreneurs to work toward the potential of being funded. This business model encourages participants to fine-tune their skills and perfect their plans before pitching to mentors and investors from across the world.
